WHAT ARE THE

PROS AND CONS?

Pros

There are various benefits of playing video games. Not everything about video games is a large negative for anyone. Here are the Top 3 Reasons why video games are beneficial.

Top 3 Benefits

Video Games encourage teamwork. In certain types of games, multiplayer is a feature where other players around the world can play with each other in the same game. This means many people can interact with those other players. This could also mean people can work together in order to complete an objective. These communication and teamwork skills can transfer into the real world. According to the National Library of Medicine, they state Moreover, the team attributes developed during this process can and do carry over into their cooperative work in high-tech, cross-functional, team-centered workplaces.

Video games trains problem solving skills. Some games require the user to solve problems in order to complete a quest or move onto another section of the game. With games constantly prompting the user to solve their problems, it can cause them to get used to the process of processing a problem, then working their way to solve the problem. According to the Fuller Youth Institute they state, Problem solving, in particular, has stood out to researchers as a unique benefit gaming offers since many games build lots of smaller challenges into larger tasks.

Video games can improve the mood of the player Video games can be a great way to help relieve stress for many people. Video games are meant to be entertaining for people and having people play their favorite games can improve their mood. According to the University of Oxford, they state Players experiencing genuine enjoyment from the games experience more positive well-being.

Other Benefits

  1. Making friends online
  2. Improved reaction times
  3. Improve concentration
  4. Allow educational games become more exciting and engaging

Cons

However, there are plenty of downsides when playing video games which could lead to problems in the long run such as health problems or injuries.

Top 3 Negatives

Video games could cause excessive weight gain. People could gain a lot more weight than usual typically because they don't spend time exercising and more time looking at the screen. In addition, people may over eat or eat a lot of unhealthy foods while gaming which could contribute to the excessive weight gain. According to Harvard University they have stated, Gaming is also associated with obesity in teens and, plausibly, the same would be shown in adults, if studied. This is due to the obvious phenomenon that if a teen is sitting in front of a screen for hours every day, he or she isn’t getting much exercise.

Video games can be VERY addicting. Considering how entertaining video games can be, people could not get enough of games. Even if hours or days have gone by, people cannot get enough of playing video games which have huge side effects socially, mentally, and/or physically. According to Harvard University, they say that video game addiction could lead to withdrawal, loss of interest in other activities, downplaying use, and loss of relationship, education, or career opportunities. People who are addicted to video games need to find a way to reduce the amount of playtime as possible.

Video games can lower self-esteem. Although that video games can help make friends online and make interaction a lot simplier, cyberbullying is another factor when interacting with others online. Furthermore, many gamers take themselves too seriously whenever nothing goes right for them. According to Family Addiction Specialist, they have stated Video game addiction has been found to be related to personality traits such as low self-esteem, low self-efficacy, attention problems, impulsivity, aggression, anxiety, and depression.

Other Negatives

  1. Carpal Tunnel/Hand Injuries
  2. Lack of sleep
  4. Anger issues
